Biography

I am currently a Ph.D. Student at the School of Computer Science, The University of Sydney, feeling fortunate to be advised by Prof. Tongliang Liu. Before, I studied Industrial Design at CUG, and then jumped to Computer Science at NJUST. I hope to achieve general machine intelligence which can tackle various real-world problems. To do that, I focus on OOD Generalization and AI Safety.
