Biography

I am currently a 3rd-year M.S. Student at PCA lab, School of Computer Science and Technology, NJUST. My quest is to enhance robust machine learning algorithms in the weakly-supervised, non-stationary, and undiscovered real-world. Specifically, my research focuses on Weakly Supervised Learning, Domain Adaptation, OOD Detection, and Causal Inference.
