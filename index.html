Biography

I am currently a Ph.D. Student at the School of Computer Science, The University of Sydney, feeling fortunate to learn from Prof. Tongliang Liu. Before, I studied Industrial Design at CUG and then jumped to Computer Science at NJUST, advised by Prof. Chen Gong. I was a visiting scholar at USTC and THU in 2022 and 2023, respectively. I hope to achieve general machine intelligence which can tackle various real-world problems. To do that, I focus on OOD Generalization and AI Safety.
