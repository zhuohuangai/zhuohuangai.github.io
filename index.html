Biography

I am currently a 1st-year Ph.D. Student at the School of Computer Science, The University of Sydney (USYD). I aim to design robust machine learning models capable of learning under distribution shifts and functioning in complicated real-world scenarios. Specifically, my research focuses on OOD Generalization, Weakly-Supervised Learning, and Causality.
